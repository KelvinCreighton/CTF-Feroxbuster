Simple:
feroxbuster -u <URL> -w <wordlist>

Low Impact:
feroxbuster -u <URL> -w <wordlist> -t <threads default=50> --rate-limit <requests_per_second> -n <--no-recursion>
Example: feroxbuster -u <URL> -w <wordlist> -t 5 --rate-limit 20 -n



Pipe urls with code 200 to a file:
| tee >(grep "http" | awk '{print $NF}' > urls.txt)
